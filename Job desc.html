Job Description

Project Manager – Full-Time / On-Site

About the Role

We are looking for a passionate Project Manager to join our creative team at DenoMax / Studio K Productions. The role involves working on high-profile projects including studio shoots, events, and commercial campaigns. You’ll collaborate with our creative directors and editors to deliver high-quality visuals that align with our brand identity.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and execute studio and on-location photo shoots.
  • Collaborate with the creative team on concept development.
  • Edit and retouch images to professional standards.
  • Maintain photography equipment and studio setup.
  • Ensure all content meets brand guidelines.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a professional photographer (portfolio required).
  • Proficiency in Adobe Photoshop / Lightroom.
  • Strong understanding of studio lighting setups.
  • Excellent time management and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary based on experience.
  • Creative, collaborative studio environment.
  • Opportunities for growth and professional development.
  • Access to industry events and exclusive projects.
